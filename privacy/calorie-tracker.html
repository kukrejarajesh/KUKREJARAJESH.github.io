Privacy Policy

📵 Calorie Tracker — Offline & Private
Last updated: April 24, 2026
The short version: Calorie Tracker works 100% offline. All your data stays on your device. We collect nothing, share nothing, and track nothing.

Overview

Calorie Tracker — Offline & Private is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app works completely offline and all data remains on your device at all times. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no data leaving your phone.

Data Collection

Calorie Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including your profile, food logs, calorie targets, and nutritional data — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

Third Party Access

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries integrated into this app.

Internet Access

This app does not require or use an internet connection. No data is sent to any server at any time.

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite — a secure, industry-standard local database. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your Android device settings.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this document.
