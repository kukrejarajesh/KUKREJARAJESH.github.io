What's Inside

Nothing you don't.

A focused, clutter-free calorie tracker built for everyday Indian meals.

🍽️

Food Logging

Log meals across Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner and Snacks from a curated Indian food database with accurate nutrition data.

🔍

Smart Search

Quickly find foods by name. Gluten-free filter is on by default — keeping unsafe foods out of your log automatically.

🏃

Exercise Logging

Log workouts and track calories burned using MET-based calculations tailored to your body weight.

💧

Water Tracking

Set a daily hydration goal and log intake in millilitres with a simple, visual tracker.

📈

Weight Trends

Log your weight daily and see your progress over time with clean, readable charts.

🎯

TDEE-Based Goals

Set personalised calorie targets using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula with goals for loss, maintain, or gain.

📊

Macro Breakdown

Track protein, carbohydrates and fat daily with visual progress bars against your personalised targets.

🌿

Gluten-Free Friendly

Every food is tagged with its gluten status. The app defaults to gluten-safe search so you never log a risky item by accident.

🌙

Light & Dark Mode

Follows your device system theme automatically. Looks great in both light and dark environments.

Your Data

Private by design.

All your data — meals, weight, water, exercise — lives only on your phone. No account required, no data uploaded, no analytics, no ads. Ever.
